Projects:

Kampus Hacks

An application that I developed with a group at Startup Weekend York Region. Utilizes speech recognition and machine learning to transcribe long speech(lectures, conferences etc.) then summarize them in a short paragraph or translate them all together into another language. Our idea was well received and we ended up winning 2nd place.

SmartPass

An app and an website I develped with my group at MHacks 8. It uses facial recognition, speech recognition and machine learning to check people into events by scanning their faces. Optionally, it will require them to say a keyphrase for secondary verification.

Alerti

An Android app I created that uses Google Maps API and GPS Tracking to find my location, compare it with my bus stop's location in varying time intervals, then alert me when I have reached my bus stop.

Everdo

An To-do list app on Android that syncs with Google Spreadsheets and Evernote.
